Frequently Asked Questions

Will it be available everywhere?

▼

Yes. The Dakota Sky Training System will be accessible from any device with internet access. You can watch on your phone, tablet, or computer, and progress is synced across all devices.

What about refunds?

▼

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the course, we'll refund your money, no questions asked.

Is this just for Pudelpointers?

▼

While designed specifically for Pudelpointers, these fundamentals apply to most hunting and gun dog breeds. The training principles are universal.

What if I already have a dog?

▼

Many modules can be adapted for adult dogs. We include sections on transitioning older dogs to new training methods.

Is there live support?

▼

Founding members get access to monthly live Q&A sessions with Glen and Stacey. Public launch members have access to our support team via email.

▼

Founding members who join during beta get 40% off lifetime access. Join the waitlist now to get notified when beta opens.
