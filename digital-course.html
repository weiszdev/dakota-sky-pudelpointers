Why We're Building This

Professional gun dog training doesn't come cheap. Sending your dog to a professional trainer typically costs $1,200 to $1,500 per month, and many owners don't have access to trainers who specialize in Pudelpointers or their specific hunting style.

We've spent over 20 years refining our training methods and working with hundreds of dogs. We know there's a better way — one that puts the training directly in your hands, with guidance from us every step of the way.

This isn't a basic "tricks and obedience" course. It's a complete, systematic approach to developing a finished hunting dog. Every module is built on principles we've tested thousands of times in the field.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the course be available everywhere?

+

Yes. The course will be accessible on any device — desktop, tablet, or smartphone. You can stream videos and access guides whenever and wherever you need them.

Can I get a refund?

+

Yes. Founding members receive a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If the course doesn't meet your expectations, we'll provide a full refund within the first 30 days of access.

Is this just for Pudelpointers?

+

The course is designed specifically for Pudelpointers, but the principles and methods apply to all hunting dogs. Pointer and setter owners will find it highly valuable.

What if I already have a dog?

+

The curriculum covers all ages, from puppies through adult dogs. Whether you're starting with an 8-week-old puppy or a 3-year-old dog, you'll find modules and techniques tailored to your situation.

Will there be live support?

+

Absolutely. Founding members get access to monthly Q&A sessions with Glen and Stacey, a community forum for peer support, and email support for specific questions about your training.

What's the founding member discount?

+

We'll announce the exact discount closer to launch, but founding members will lock in a lifetime rate — meaning you'll never pay more, even if the course price increases in the future.
