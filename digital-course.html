Why We're Building This

Good gun dog training is expensive. A professional trainer runs $1,200–$1,500 per month. That's why most owners never get the professional training their dog needs.

But there's another way. Hundreds of hunters successfully train their own dogs every year. They just need the right guidance — and that's what this course is.

The Dakota Sky Training System brings Glen and Stacey's 20+ years of experience directly to you. You'll get the same methods we use with our training dogs, adapted for home trainers who want professional results.

This isn't a "learn dog training basics" course. It's a complete, systematic approach to taking your puppy from day one through finished gun dog — with video, detailed guides, and real-world troubleshooting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the course be available everywhere?
Yes. The course will be available on a platform you can access from any device — smartphone, tablet, laptop, desktop. Stream or download videos.
Can I get a refund if it's not right for me?
Founding members who opt in early will have a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If the course doesn't work for you, we'll refund you in full.
Is this just for Pudelpointers?
The course is specifically designed for Pudelpointers, but the principles apply to any hunting dog breed. Glen trains many breeds — Labradors, Brittanys, Pointers, and more.
What if I already have a dog? Can I still use the course?
Absolutely. Modules will cover training dogs at different ages and experience levels — from 8-week puppies through adult dogs with bad habits.
Will there be live support or Q&A?
Yes. Founding members will get access to monthly Q&A sessions with Glen and Stacey. Later we'll add community forums and email support.
What's the founding member discount?
We'll announce exact pricing closer to launch. But early subscribers will lock in a special lifetime rate — a permanent discount you keep forever.
