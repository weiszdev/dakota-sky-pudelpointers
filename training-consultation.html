Glen and Stacey have spent over two decades developing a deep understanding of what it takes to bring out the best in hunting dogs. Their approach combines time-tested training methods with personalized attention to each dog's unique personality and potential. Whether your goal is to create a world-class hunting partner or a well-behaved family companion, they have the expertise to get you there.

We believe in fueling every aspect of your dog's development, which is why we partnered with Inukshuk nutrition to provide premium nutrition plans alongside our training programs. A well-fed dog is a well-trained dog. Your consultation will cover not just training methodology, but also how proper nutrition supports your dog's learning and performance in the field.

Training doesn't end when your dog leaves our facility. We provide ongoing support, answer questions, and help you navigate the journey of maintaining and building on the foundation we've created together. Our goal is your success and your dog's happiness.
