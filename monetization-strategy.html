Competitive Advantage

What Makes Dakota Sky Unique

  • Reputation & Personal Touch: Glen and Stacey's 20+ years of hands-on experience and local credibility
  • Location: Heart of hunting country (North Dakota/Upper Midwest)
  • Inukshuk Dealership: Only local dealer in region — unique partnership opportunity
  • Bundled Offerings: Nobody else packages training + nutrition + consulting with puppies
  • Digital Course Gap: No Pudelpointer-specific online training exists — first-mover advantage
  • Multi-Channel Revenue: Diversified beyond just puppies and training

Challenges to Address

  • Marketing: Small team, limited marketing budget. Need leveraging through content + word-of-mouth
  • Scale Ceiling: Training and boarding limited by physical capacity. Solve with digital courses
  • Geographic: Rural North Dakota limits walk-in traffic. Solve with email, social, online sales
  • Course Production: Building digital course requires time investment upfront
  • Hiring: If growth outpaces capacity, will need to hire trainers or administrators
