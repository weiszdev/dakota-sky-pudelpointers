Our Breeding Standards

Every dog at Dakota Sky undergoes rigorous evaluation. We don't just breed—we curate bloodlines that combine exceptional field performance with stable, family-friendly temperaments.

Health Testing

OFA hips and elbows, cardiac evaluation, eye certification. Every breeding animal must pass comprehensive health protocols before participating in our program.

NAVHDA Testing

North American Versatile Hunting Dog Association certification ensures field performance across all game types: upland, waterfowl, and furred game.

Temperament Evaluation

We assess personality, trainability, and home suitability. A brilliant hunter with a poor temperament doesn't make the cut for Dakota Sky breeding.

Four Pillars

Disposition, Conformation, Hunting Ability, Soundness—every breeding dog must excel in all four categories.

The Puppy Process

Getting a Dakota Sky puppy is straightforward and transparent. Here's what to expect:

1

Submit Application

Fill out our detailed application. We want to understand your hunting experience, living situation, and goals for a Pudelpointer.

2

Interview & Waitlist

We'll call to discuss your situation and answer questions. Upon approval, you're placed on our waitlist for your preferred litter.

3

Litter Announcement

When puppies arrive, we announce the litter and begin the matching process. Photos and updates come weekly during the raising period.

4

Puppy Evaluation

At 6-7 weeks, we assess temperament and hunting drive. We'll discuss which puppy best fits your family and hunting goals.

5

Go-Home Day

At 8 weeks, your puppy comes home with our Premium Puppy Package including initial training, nutrition, and lifetime breeder support.
