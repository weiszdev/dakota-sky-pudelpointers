Our Breeding Standards

We don't breed puppies — we produce hunting companions and family members. Every breeding is guided by OFA health testing, NAVHDA field evaluation, rigorous temperament assessment, and our four pillars: Disposition, Conformation, Hunting Ability, and Soundness.

The Puppy Process

1

Apply

Submit your application and tell us about your family and hunting goals.

2

Interview

We discuss your needs and match you with the right dog for your lifestyle.

3

Announcement

Litter arrives and we notify approved applicants of available puppies.

4

Evaluation

At 7 weeks, puppies are temperament tested to match families.

5

Go-Home Day

Your puppy joins your family at 8–9 weeks with lifetime support.
