Our Facility

Located on 10 acres in Dickinson, our boarding facility is specifically designed for active hunting dogs. We maintain climate-controlled kennels with both indoor and outdoor access, allowing dogs to stay comfortable year-round across North Dakota's seasonal extremes.

Every dog receives individualized attention from our experienced staff. We keep detailed records of health, behavior, and activity—because we understand your dog is family.
