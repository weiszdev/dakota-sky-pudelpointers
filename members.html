Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly am I paying for? +

You're joining an exclusive membership community that gives you access to premium benefits worth over $199 monthly. This includes discounts on Inukshuk nutrition, early access to new hunting products, merchandise discounts, exclusive podcast episodes, behind-the-scenes kennel content, and the DSP Training Guide. All for just $9.95 a month.

Is there a long-term contract or commitment? +

No contracts required. Your membership is month-to-month. You can cancel anytime without penalties. We believe if you're getting real value, you'll want to stay. Contact us if you have any issues.

Do I have to be a DSP puppy owner? +

No. While DSP puppy owners do get complimentary access to the Training Guide, the membership is open to anyone passionate about hunting and interested in our kennel's insights and products. Serious hunters of all backgrounds are welcome.

When do I get access to the benefits? +

You'll receive immediate access to all membership benefits after completing your signup. You'll get instructions to access the private podcast feed, member-only content portal, and information on claiming your discounts on Inukshuk and DSP merchandise.

How does the 10% Inukshuk discount work? +

As a member, you'll receive a discount code to apply to any Inukshuk subscription order. The 10% savings applies to your monthly deliveries for as long as you're an active member. It stacks on top of any other promotions Inukshuk may be running.

Can I gift a membership? +

Absolutely! Membership gift codes are available. Contact us at dakotasky.nd@gmail.com or call (701) 290-1492 to purchase gift codes for fellow hunters who would appreciate DSP Insider benefits.

What if I have issues with billing? +

We're here to help. Contact Glen and Stacey directly if you have any concerns about your account, billing, or membership. Call (701) 290-1492 or email dakotasky.nd@gmail.com and we'll get it sorted quickly.
