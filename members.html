Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly am I paying for?

▼

Your membership provides access to exclusive content, early product releases, special discounts on nutrition and merchandise, and behind-the-scenes insights into how we breed, train, and develop our Pudelpointers. You're investing in a community of serious hunters who share your passion.

Is there a contract or commitment?

▼

No contracts. Your membership is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. We're confident the value will keep you coming back, but we respect your freedom to opt out whenever you choose.

Do I need to be a Dakota Sky puppy owner?

▼

Not at all. Our Insiders community welcomes anyone passionate about hunting dogs, whether or not you own a DSP dog. You'll connect with fellow hunters, learn from our experience, and get exclusive content and deals.

When do I get access?

▼

Access begins immediately upon signup. You'll receive an email confirmation with login details and can start exploring member-only content right away.

How does the Inukshuk discount work?

▼

Members receive 10% off every Inukshuk subscription order. Your discount applies automatically at checkout. Multi-dog households can stack discounts with our volume pricing.

Can I gift membership to someone?

▼

Yes. Contact us at hello@dakotaskypdp.com and we can arrange a gift membership. It's a perfect present for fellow hunting enthusiasts.

What if I have billing issues?

▼

We're here to help. Reach out to hello@dakotaskypdp.com with any billing questions or concerns. We'll work with you to resolve any issues quickly.
