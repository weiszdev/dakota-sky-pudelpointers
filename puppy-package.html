Why Bundle?

Training Matters Early

A puppy's first year establishes foundational habits and behaviors that last a lifetime. Professional training during this critical period ensures your Dakota Sky Pudelpointer develops properly. Early habituation, basic commands, and correct socialization prevent behavioral issues before they start.

Nutrition = Development

Premium nutrition during growth is non-negotiable. A year of Inukshuk provides consistent, high-quality fuel for muscle development, joint health, and coat quality. Switching foods mid-development disrupts digestive systems and can compromise development. Bundled nutrition ensures continuity.

You Save Money

Bundle pricing provides significant savings. The Hunter's Start package saves you $600 compared to purchasing services separately. The Complete Companion saves $2,200. When you invest in complete preparation upfront, you invest in years of reliability and partnership with your dog.
