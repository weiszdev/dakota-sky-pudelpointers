Our Philosophy

We built Dakota Sky on four pillars: disposition, conformation, hunting ability, and soundness. Every dog in our program earns its place through proven performance in the field and proven temperament in the home. Glen and Stacey Koffler have dedicated their lives to producing the finest versatile hunting companions in the Northern Plains.

The Breed

What Makes a Pudelpointer?

A rare German gun dog combining the pointing instinct of the English Pointer with the intelligence and water aptitude of the Standard Poodle. Wire-coated, weather-resistant, and remarkably smart — the ultimate versatile hunting companion.
