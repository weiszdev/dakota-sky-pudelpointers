About Dakota Sky Pudelpointers

Founded by Glen and Stacey Koffler, Dakota Sky Pudelpointers is built on more than 20 years of experience raising and breeding exceptional hunting dogs. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond breeding—we test all our parent dogs through NAVHDA, ensuring they possess not only the physical attributes but also the temperament, drive, and intelligence that define the Pudelpointer breed.

Every puppy that leaves Dakota Sky is trained with the same dedication, care, and proven methods we've refined over decades. We don't just breed puppies; we raise future hunting partners and cherished family members. Our mission is to equip every new owner with the knowledge and tools they need to unlock their Pudelpointer's full potential.
