About Dakota Sky Pudelpointers

Glen and Stacey Koffler have been breeding and training Pudelpointers for over 20 years at Dakota Sky, a premium hunting dog kennel in Dickinson, North Dakota.

Every puppy from Dakota Sky comes from NAVHDA-tested parents and is raised with the same care and attention to detail that goes into our training dogs. We don't just breed puppies — we prepare them for success in the field and in your home.

This free guide represents the wisdom Glen and Stacey have gained from raising hundreds of puppies. It's the same foundation we use at Dakota Sky, and now you can apply it to your own dog.

Whether you're getting a Dakota Sky puppy or any Pudelpointer, this guide gives you the roadmap to a confident, well-trained hunting companion.
