The Founders

A Journey That Began 20+ Years Ago

Glen and Stacey discovered Pudelpointers over two decades ago while hunting across the Northern Plains. Captivated by the breed's versatility, intelligence, and steady temperament, they built their lives around these remarkable dogs. In 2010, they founded Dakota Sky — a small, purpose-driven breeding program dedicated to raising world-class Pudelpointers.

They personally raise every litter, mentor every puppy owner, and maintain lifetime relationships with their dogs. For Glen and Stacey, Dakota Sky isn't a business — it's a life calling.

Where Excellence Grows

Our Land

Situated on 10 acres off Highway 10 in Dickinson, North Dakota, Dakota Sky's facility is purpose-built for breeding and training excellence. Our land features diverse terrain — bird fields, ponds, spacious kennels, and training grounds — where every dog develops the skills and confidence they need to thrive in the field and beyond.

This is where puppies first learn to hunt, where breeding stock is evaluated, and where lifelong partnerships with our dogs begin.

The Dakota Sky Guarantee

Every puppy that leaves our care comes with our lifetime commitment. This isn't just a warranty — it's a promise to be your partner every step of your dog's life.

2-Year Health Guarantee

Every puppy is health tested and guaranteed against hereditary conditions. If issues arise within two years, we'll work with you — including replacement options.

Initial Training Included

Foundational puppy training and socialization begin at our facility. Your puppy arrives ready to bond and learn in their new home.

3 Months Inukshuk Support

Every puppy starts on our premium nutrition partnership. We provide nutrition guidance and support through the critical growth phase.

Lifetime Support

Questions about training, health, behavior, or breeding? Glen and Stacey are here — for the life of your dog. We answer calls, give advice, and celebrate successes.

Right of First Refusal

If your dog's life circumstances change, Dakota Sky has first right to take the dog back — no questions asked.

Breeding Program Access

If your Dakota Sky Pudelpointer is exceptional, we'd like to know. We collaborate with owners on future breeding opportunities.
