What Makes a Pudelpointer?

The Pudelpointer is a rare German gun dog that combines the best of two worlds: the pointing instinct and athleticism of the English Pointer with the intelligence and water aptitude of the Standard Poodle. The result? A versatile hunter that excels on upland birds, waterfowl, and even tracking furred game—all while maintaining an exceptional temperament in the home.
