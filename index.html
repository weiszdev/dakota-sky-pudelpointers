Our Philosophy

We built Dakota Sky on four pillars: disposition, conformation, hunting ability, and soundness. Every dog in our program earns its place through proven performance in the field and proven temperament in the home.

Glen and Stacey Koffler have dedicated their lives to producing the finest versatile hunting companions in the Northern Plains. With decades of experience and an unwavering commitment to excellence, we don't just breed dogs—we develop partners.

What Makes a Pudelpointer?

The Pudelpointer is a rare German gun dog that combines the best of two worlds: the pointing instinct and athleticism of the English Pointer with the intelligence and water aptitude of the Standard Poodle. The result? A versatile hunter that excels on upland birds, waterfowl, and even tracking furred game—all while maintaining an exceptional temperament in the home.
